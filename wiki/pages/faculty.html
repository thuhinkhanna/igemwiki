Dr. Gayathri V

Dr. Gayathri V


Primary Principal Investigator

Behind every successful team is a strong leader with a vision. A leader is the one who knows the way, goes the way and shows the way. She is a firm believer of “nothing can possibly encourage a person to learn more than when they are teaching and sharing the knowledge with others”. She has always been a seeker and has never stepped aside from motivating her students to seek. This practice of hers has turned her students to be curious learners. She holds phenomenal expertise in various fields like Biochemistry, Food technology, Marine Biology and Environmental Biotechnology. She successfully holds 2 patents under her name. She has been a part of the institution for over 2 decades and since then she has instilled in the young student minds the fact “Being small cannot stop us from thinking big”. REC-CHENNAI takes utmost pride in introducing its very beloved mentor, guide and Primary Principal Investigator Dr.V.Gayathri.
Dr. Jayasree R

Dr. Jayasree R


Secondary Principal Investigator

Great leaders keep perspective, maintain clarity and remain calm when the storm brews", is a quote that perfectly describes Dr. Jayasree, who is always found with a calm and comforting smile. She is one of the institute's highly sought after and cherished professors. A doctorate with 2 decades of teaching experience under her crown, her expertise is spread across Biochemistry, Genetics and molecular biology, Phytochemistry and Environmental Biotechnology. She is fondly referred to as an RJ by her friends for her exceptional literary skills. She has invariably persuaded her students to be curious about everything around them. She stands as an example by spreading her wings across classical music and dance. Her advice to her students is to develop a thirst for knowledge and inculcate a mindset that turns them into a lifelong learner. Team REC-CHENNAI feels blessed to have Dr. Jayasree as its Secondary Principal Investigator, an amazing guide with a strong and positive mindset.

Ms. Shreenidhi K.S

Ms. Shreenidhi K.S


Instructor

A Professor knows what she’s doing, but it takes true talent for a mentor to know what her students are doing. Yes, that’s Ms. Shreenidhi. She is the department’s toxicologist, specialized in Environmental Biotechnology, currently pursuing her Ph. D in the field of Biotechnology. Just like how Charles Xavier guides his students, Ms. Shreenidhi steers the igemers of REC-CHENNAI with her knowledge, care and experience. She has an ability to understand each student from their perspective. She is someone who never takes ‘no’ for an answer. She encourages the team and pushes them towards the right path. When the team feels down, we don’t head to our psychologists but to her. Students find solace in her at times of disappointment in any project result. On par with google, Ms. Shreenidhi always comes up with innovative solutions for the team’s troubleshooting. Even as a professor, her classes are always a highly anticipated hour. She never fails to entertain her students amidst her lectures with fun quizzes and games. At times of fun and relaxation, she brings out the child in her, expressing her playful nature. She always believes that teaching is not a job but a lifestyle. If there’s something that she abides by and lives by, its “I have promises to keep, And miles to go before I sleep, Miles to go before I sleep.” With this, Team REC-CHENNAI is thrilled to introduce it's team instructor for the year 2022 iGEM, Ms. Shreenidhi K S.
