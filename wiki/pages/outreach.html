Beach Outreach Program

iGEM REC Chennai team had the beautiful opportunity to organize an outreach program in Edwards Elliot beach, Chennai. Our team interacted with the general public by educating them about what heavy metals are and to what extent can they cause harm. The people we had to interact with, were extremely patient and had the understanding to listen to what we iGEMers had to say. They also took part in the games we had planned for them after listening to us. The game that we curated was called “Tint in the Ball”. The concept behind this game is simple, throw the ball that we give you into any of the 4 baskets that were placed. These baskets had been assigned to one particular food item.


The food options were carrots, chicken, fish, and rice. You have to throw the ball into the basket within 2 turns. If you do so, you win and get an amazing bookmark that was designed and made by our very own creative team. Apart from this, we also enlightened the players about how heavy metals can impact the food chain and how that, in turn, can drastically affect our lives and the environment. We had the opportunity to interact with over 70+ people and let them know how they can help keep this planet and themselves safe from long-term exposure to heavy metals. We also conducted surveys to gauge information about the existing awareness of heavy metals among the general public.


The survey was aimed at gathering information on various aspects of heavy metals and how people are contributing to a safer and cleaner environment. This survey helped us understand that more than 70% of the people were not aware of heavy metals and how harmful they are, and the most worrying part was that people seemed shocked when they came to know about the various sources of heavy metals and how they can infiltrate our food chain. This survey was an eye-opener for us as it motivated us to keep spreading our concern and need for a safer and healthier life.



Interactive Awareness Program

Team iGEM REC Chennai designed and crafted an enjoyable and entertaining mobile app game titled “Curcoli”. We organized an inter-college Game launch event where all the students and professors belonging to various departments were invited and welcomed. This game holds a special place in our hearts as this was one of the earliest ideas proposed by the team that was brought to life. This game is designed to help people understand some microorganisms that require attention. This game also contains fun facts relevant to our project.


This awareness session helped various students and professors understand the importance of our project and how is it that using E.coli helps us achieve our goals. This session also was an excellent opportunity for us to introduce our team that is going to be representing iGEM-REC Chennai to our fellow students and professors. Life surely becomes more fun when you play games. So go ahead and play our game while having fun learning! Happy Gaming!
